We all have experience with Covid 19

But what exactly is it?

Coronaviruses are a type of virus. There are many different kinds, and some cause disease. It is more contagious than the flu. It also has a wide range of symptoms that present challenges from mild to fatal not limited to:

  • Fever or chills
  • Cough
  • Fatigue
  • Loss of taste or smell
